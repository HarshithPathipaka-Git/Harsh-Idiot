# Text Elements

## `<h1>` – `<h6>`

**Purpose:** Headings (largest → smallest)

**Use:** Page titles and section headings.

⭐ Use only one `<h1>` per page for SEO.

---

## `<p>`

**Purpose:** Paragraph

**Use:** Normal text content.

---

## `<br>`

**Purpose:** Line break

**Use:** Move text to the next line.

⚠️ Don't use for spacing.

---

## `<hr>`

**Purpose:** Horizontal divider

**Use:** Separate sections.

---

## `<strong>`

**Purpose:** Important text

**Default:** Bold

⭐ Semantic meaning (better than `<b>`).

---

## `<em>`

**Purpose:** Emphasized text

**Default:** Italic

⭐ Semantic meaning (better than `<i>`).

---

## `<b>`

**Purpose:** Bold text

**Use:** Visual styling only.

---

## `<i>`

**Purpose:** Italic text

**Use:** Visual styling only.

---

## `<u>`

**Purpose:** Underlined text

**Use:** Highlight text.

⚠️ Avoid for hyperlinks.

---

## `<mark>`

**Purpose:** Highlight text

**Default:** Yellow background.

---

## `<small>`

**Purpose:** Smaller text

**Use:** Copyright, notes, disclaimers.

---

## `<sub>`

**Purpose:** Subscript

**Example:** H₂O

---

## `<sup>`

**Purpose:** Superscript

**Example:** x²

---

## `<del>`

**Purpose:** Deleted text

**Default:** Strike-through.

---

## `<ins>`

**Purpose:** Inserted text

**Default:** Underlined.

---

## `<code>`

**Purpose:** Inline code

**Example:**

```html
<code>console.log()</code>
```

---

## `<pre>`

**Purpose:** Preformatted text

**Use:** Preserve spaces and line breaks.

Great for code blocks.

---

## `<blockquote>`

**Purpose:** Long quotation

**Use:** Quotes from books, articles, websites.

---

## `<q>`

**Purpose:** Short inline quotation.

Example:

"He said Hello"

---

## `<abbr>`

**Purpose:** Abbreviation

Example:

```html
<abbr title="HyperText Markup Language">HTML</abbr>
```

---

## `<cite>`

**Purpose:** Reference a creative work

Examples:

- Book
    
- Movie
    
- Song
    
- Research Paper
    

---

## `<address>`

**Purpose:** Contact information

Examples:

- Email
    
- Phone
    
- Company Address
    
- Website
    

---

# ⭐ Most Used in Full Stack

- h1–h6
    
- p
    
- strong
    
- em
    
- code
    
- pre
    
- br
    
- hr
    

# ⭐ Occasionally Used

- small
    
- sub
    
- sup
    
- del
    
- ins

- blockquote
    
- q
    
- abbr
    
- cite
